- **Description:** The slide features a title "Incentives vs. Forcing Functions" and a brief explanation of the two concepts. It states that both shape behavior but in different ways. - **Timestamp:** 5:51 - 8:07
- **Description:** The slide features the title "Environmental Forcing Functions" and explains that these are natural cycles and variations that compel adaptations within ecosystems.     - **Deciduous Trees:** Adapt to seasonal changes by shedding leaves in autumn.
